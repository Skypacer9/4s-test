"Dinogender" (Xenogender)

- **Dinogender** is a type of _xenogender_ where someone feels an extremely strong connection to dinosaurs, or uses them to better understand their gender identity. 
- It is used by those who might feel that traditional human gender concepts do not fit them. Someone who is dinogender might use terms like "dinoboy," "dinogirl," or "dinoenby".
